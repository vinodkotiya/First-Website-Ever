Hi All,

I receive many mails regarding GATE preps. Here are some tips that might answer your questions as well....What follows are my suggestions and do take it as a friend's advice- I take no resposiblities ;-)

Many students keep asking me about gate, hence i decided to write this page to help them out..

1.Read text books only. dont read anything else. dont read any notes, any brililant tutorials thing.
trust the text books, ur knowledge and ur own judgement.
2.buy any book in the market which has got old GATE papers. do not trust the answers given in those books,most of them are wrong!
3. u may but u need not go for the extra topicwise que. given in those books, again dont trust those que. and those answers, u may use them for practise only.
4. u r ur own judge, ask teachers in case of doubt.
5. duration: i did it for 15-17 days.( but i prepared very rigorously during those days) some of my seniors also did it for the same period. If u think u have studied in last 2 years and u have understood the subjects then that much period should be sufficient. to be on safer side, u may prepare for one month. otherwise if u think that u dont know the things then prepare for whole year
6. what is imp in gate is how much u can solbe in those 180 minutes. practise of solving papers is very imp. solve 2-3 of them before exam. u can solve one or two now to judge urself.

1. Which books should be followed for GATE and what subjects should we concentrate on?

For GATE preps, you can refer to the following Texts:

1. Basic Mathematics : It is not req. much in GATE, just numerical
maths is req. and you can use Rajaraman's Book which is short and
concise..that is suff., do Basic maths in the last month..
2. Discrete Mathematics
Tremblay & Manohar
C.L. Liu
Prepare this subject well...it has lot of weightage..
3. Theory of Computation
Hopcroft and Ullman (for problem solving)
John C. Martin (for Theory)
Again, this one has lot of weightage..Prepare DM and TOC very well...start
NOW!

4. computer hardware
Not required..

5. Computer Organization
Hayes
OR
Hennesy Patterson (Hardware and Software Interface)

Usually Objective type questions are asked...

6. microprocessor (8085)
Gaonkar

7. Programming and Data Structures
Pratt's book abt Programming language is a useful reference..
Book by Mark A. Weiss is amongst the best book I came across for Algos and
data structures..

8. Language Processors
Tremblay Sorenson or
Aho, Ullman and Sethi (preferable)

9. OS
Silbershatz
Refer to Tanenbaum also if you want..

UNIX Internals
The Design of UNIX Operating System- Maurice Bach

10. DBMS
Korth, Silbershatz and Sudershan is good enough...

11. Digital Electronics..
Morris Mano
good subject to score quick marks...

Q2. How to manage time in GATE?
A. Try to do 6-8 questions problems correctly than trying to attempt everything.

All the very best for preparations...
Jignashu - Banglore
