Mathematical magics

 

1) Application of the formulas:

 

  We know the frequently used mathematical formula

 

              a2 –b2 = (a +b)* (a-b). Here that lists the applications of it in squaring.

 

 Suppose you need to find the square of the number around 100. Let it be X.

 

Step 1:  “Add or Subtract a number(Y) from the original number(X), by the same

amount by which X is above or below  from 100 respectively.  Let the     resultant number be Z

      Step 2:   Find the square of Y.

      Step 3:   Suffix Y2 as a 2 digit number with Z

      Step 4:  Square of X = ZY2

 

Example:

 

1.        97 2  = (97+3)*(97-3)  + 3 2

Here subtraction is performed

Here X=97   Y= 100-97 =3   Z=97-3 =94

   Y2=09     X2 = ZY2 =9409.

 

2.        1072 = (100+7)*(100-7) + 72

Here addition is performed

Here X= 107  Y= 107-100 =7   Z= 107 + 7 = 114

   Y2 = 49   X2= ZY2 = 11449.

 

 

2) Squaring a number around 50

 

      Let  X be the number (around 50) you need to find the square .

 

Formula :  (a+b)2 = a2 +b2+2*a*b   (a-b)2 = a2 + b2 + 2*a*b

   For   a=50  the formula reduces to

   (50+b)2 = (25+b)*100 + b2      (50-b)2 = (25-b)*100 + b2

 

Step 1:  “Add or Subtract a number(b) from  25, by the same amount by which X is            

             above or below  from 50 respectively.  Let the resultant number be Z

      Step 2:   Find the square of b.

      Step 3:   Suffix b2 as a 2 digit number with Z

      Step 4:  Square of X = Zb2

 

Example :

1) 592 = (25+9)*100 + 92  = 3481.

 

Here X= 59  Z=25+9  = 34 b= 9

 

2)   422 = (25-8)*100 + 82  = 1764

  Here X= 42  Z= 25-8 =17    b= 8
